How to run this

  

    

      
THE LOOP — five moves, bottom bar

      
    
        
  1. A · AUTHOR — draw on the board. Add a frame to keep going.
    2. 
        
  2. B · BRANCH — tap a node to point at it. Fork to make an alternate take.
    3. 
        
  3. C · CREW — bring people in. They draw with you, live.
    4. 
        
  4. D · DIRECT — arrange every frame on the light table. Drag to reorder. The first reorder forks a TWIN so your cut never touches the original branch.
    5. 
        
  5. E · IN/OUT — publish the DIRECT cut on its own, or export the whole film.
    6. 
      

    


    
THE BOARD

      

        
Pen
Press and drag to lay dots. Pick a tone at the right of the tool row — left dots are light, right dots are heavy.

        
Box
Press one corner, drag to the other, release. Draws a rectangle outline in the current tone.

        
Erase
Press and drag to clear dots back to white.

        
Tone dots
The row of dots sets ink weight, light to dark. The swatch at the far right shows the tone you're holding.

        
Size slider
Sets how many frames per second a preview plays. Higher = faster playback.

        
Play
Runs the current path as a flip. Press again to stop. Drawing also stops it.

      

    


    
THE BREADCRUMB (above the board)

      

        
WORLD
The whole film. Tap it to aim the verbs at the top level.

        
act · scene · syntagm
The nesting under the world. Tap a level to point the verbs there.

        
frame a07
Where this frame sits in the run. The number is its position in the cut.

      

    


    
DIRECT — the light table

      

        
Five views
Grid, list, columns, board, outline — tap the icons top-left. Every one shows the frames as thumbnails.

        
Drag a frame
Reorders the cut as an overlay. The order sticks and plays back. The branch is not rewritten.

        
Tap a frame
Aims the board at that frame to edit it. Close DIRECT and you're drawing on it. The board shows its a07.

        
Play on a frame
Previews the cut from there on the board, then returns you to where you were editing.

        
DIRECT CUT
DIRECT shows the frames on the branch you're on — the same set as the timeline. Enter a different branch and DIRECT re-scopes to it. Your reorder is an overlay: the order sticks, but the branch itself is never rewritten.

        
Branch this cut
Forks your arranged order into a new branch line and drops you on its tip in the editor, so you can diverge and author more. The timeline marks lines that came from a cut.

        
Crumb
Tap WORLD to see the whole film; tap a level to narrow DIRECT to that branch.

      

    


    
TOP BAR

      

        
Mic / Camera
Turn on audio and your camera for a live session.

        
Connect
Open a room. Share the link to bring crew in.

        
Chat
Send text. Words you type become a face on the frame.

        
Log
See events and per-frame cost.

        
?
Open this sheet.

      

    


    
IN / OUT

      

        
Bring in
Open a saved project, scene pack, BEFLIX, or Twee.

        
Publish the DIRECT cut
Ships your arranged DIRECT sequence — as GIF, WEBM, Flipbook, or Twee. The branch topology is left alone.

        
Export the whole film
Saves or renders the entire tree, all branches.
